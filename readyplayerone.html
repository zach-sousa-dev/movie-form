ENTER THE OASIS
READY PLAYER ONE





"READY PLAYER ONE," DIRECTED BY STEVEN SPIELBERG AND BASED ON ERNEST CLINE'S NOVEL, IS A SCI-FI ADVENTURE SET IN 2045 WHERE TEENAGER WADE WATTS ESCAPES HIS BLEAK REALITY BY IMMERSING HIMSELF IN THE OASIS, A VAST VIRTUAL REALITY UNIVERSE. THE STORY FOLLOWS WADE, USING HIS AVATAR PARZIVAL, AS HE JOINS FORCES WITH FELLOW GAMERS TO FIND AN EASTER EGG HIDDEN BY THE OASIS'S CREATOR, JAMES HALLIDAY. THE WINNER WILL INHERIT HALLIDAY'S FORTUNE AND CONTROL OF THE OASIS. THEY MUST OUTSMART THE RUTHLESS IOI CORPORATION AND ITS LEADER, NOLAN SORRENTO, IN A THRILLING QUEST FILLED WITH 80S POP CULTURE REFERENCES AND HIGH-STAKES CHALLENGES, BOTH VIRTUAL AND REAL.
